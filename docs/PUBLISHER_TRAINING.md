# Publisher Training

Publisher Basics teaches the minimum publication rules:

1. Publish only approved video.
2. Use the assigned destination.
3. Put `tracking_link` in the post, caption, comment, or link field.
4. Submit `final_url` after publishing.
5. Do not replace the tracking link with a direct marketplace URL.

## Why Tracking Link Matters

`tracking_link` captures first-party clicks through `/r/{slug}`.

`final_url` is the public URL of the published social post.

Both are required for clean attribution:

```text
tracking_link -> clicks
final_url -> published post identity
publishing_task -> campaign / SKU / participant
```

## Certification Questions

- Can you publish `needs_human_review` video? Correct answer: no.
- What link must be placed in the post? Correct answer: `tracking_link`.
- What connects the public social post back to the system? Correct answer: `final_url` / `publishing_task`.
