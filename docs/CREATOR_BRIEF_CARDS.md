# Creator Brief Cards

Participant assignments store the operational video brief in `ParticipantAssignment.brief_json`.

The brief card includes:

- SKU and product title;
- buyer need;
- safe promise;
- hook text;
- first-frame logic;
- references;
- product identity rules;
- geometry and scale rules;
- must-include and must-avoid lists;
- output format and destination;
- review checklist;
- payout rule reference.

Briefs can be created from content runs, creative variants, publishing tasks, or product-only assignments. The goal is to make the task precise enough that creators do not improvise away from the product and campaign rules.
