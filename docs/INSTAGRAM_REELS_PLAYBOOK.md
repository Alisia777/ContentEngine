# Instagram / Reels Playbook

Use the assigned Reel task, approved video, caption, destination, and `tracking_link`.

Return:

- `final_url`
- `posted_url` or `tracking_slug`
- views
- reach
- impressions
- likes
- comments
- shares
- saves
- clicks when visible or through tracking link

Do not publish unapproved video, replace tracking link with a direct marketplace URL, use the wrong destination, or submit stats without traceability.
