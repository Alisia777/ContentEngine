# AI Brief Studio

AI Brief Studio is the editable part of the Creative Quality Workbench.

Operators can safely patch only creative brief fields:

- buyer situation;
- main objection;
- proof moment;
- CTA;
- platform angle;
- creator persona;
- product reason;
- must include;
- must avoid.

The editor must not patch provider secrets, spend gates, quality status, product lock mode, or product reference approval status. Product references still require the normal asset review flow.

## Why This Exists

The workbench turns scattered JSON into a human review flow. A content lead should be able to answer:

- what are we selling;
- to whom;
- why this buyer cares;
- what proof is shown;
- what the blogger says;
- why real smoke is allowed or blocked.

If a script has weak meaning, the right action is a brief patch or rewrite, not a paid provider call.
