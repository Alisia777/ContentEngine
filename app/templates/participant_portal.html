How to earn

PublisherEarn for traceable published posts with final_url and tracking_link.
MetricsEarn for valid reports that match posted_url or tracking_slug.
ReviewerEarn for correct approve/reject/regenerate decisions.
CreatorEarn for approved videos and accepted revisions.
OwnerEarn from placements, CPA, revenue share, or hybrid rules.

Payout is blocked when work has no assignment, no approval, no final_url, no matched metrics, wrong destination, or a direct product URL was used instead of tracking_link.
